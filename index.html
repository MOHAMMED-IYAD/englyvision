Health Dos and Don’ts

Today I will talk about some tips to stay healthy.

Food and Drink

Do

  • Eat healthy food.
  • Drink a lot of water.
  • Eat fruit and vegetables.

Don’t

  • Don’t eat a lot of fast food.
  • Don’t drink a lot of soda.
  • Don’t skip meals.

Sleep

Do

  • Sleep early at night.
  • Sleep 7 to 8 hours.
  • Take rest before sleeping.

Don’t

  • Don’t stay awake late.
  • Don’t use your phone in bed.
  • Don’t sleep very little.

Exercise

Do

  • Do exercise every week.
  • Walk or play sports.
  • Warm up before exercise.

Don’t

  • Don’t sit for a long time.
  • Don’t exercise too much.
  • Don’t exercise when you are tired.

Stress

Do

  • Take breaks.
  • Talk to your friends.
  • Listen to calm music.

Don’t

  • Don’t worry too much.
  • Don’t work for a long time.
  • Don’t keep your problems inside.

Avoiding & Recovering from Illness

Do

  • Rest when you are sick.
  • Take medicine.
  • Drink warm drinks.

Don’t

  • Don’t go out when you feel sick.
  • Don’t ignore your illness.
  • Don’t eat junk food.
